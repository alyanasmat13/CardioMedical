About Us

Cardio Medical was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate students who wanted to create a supportive community for premed students. Our mission is to provide resources, guidance, and inspiration to help future physicians navigate their journey in medicine.

Over the years, we have grown into a vibrant organization offering podcasts, databases, articles, and a dedicated team to assist students in achieving their dreams.
